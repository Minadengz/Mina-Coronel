Tower Bridge, London

Tower Bridge is one of London’s most famous landmarks and a true symbol of the city. Built between 1886 and 1894, this magnificent **bascule and suspension bridge** spans the River Thames, connecting the central and eastern parts of the city.

Tower Bridge is not only a functional crossing but also a popular attraction, offering breathtaking views of London from its **high-level walkways** and **glass floors**. Whether admiring its grand structure from the riverbanks or walking across it, this historic bridge is a must-visit destination.

Seeing Tower Bridge at Night

At night, Tower Bridge lights up beautifully, reflecting off the Thames and creating a magical view. I dream of taking an evening stroll along the river, capturing the stunning illuminated bridge, and feeling the historic charm of London at night.

One of the most exciting things I’d love to experience is walking across the **glass walkway** of Tower Bridge.
